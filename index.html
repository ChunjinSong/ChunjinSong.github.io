Biography

I am a 3rd-year PhD student at the University of British Columbia, advised by Prof. Helge Rhodin.

My research interests lie in the area of Computer Vision and Graphics. Specifically, I'm particularly interested in human avatar modeling, 3D generative models, arbitrary style transfer and image generation.
